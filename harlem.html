The Black Panthers was an example of unity that helped established the first free breakfast program. This program still continue for today.
Black Panther was created in 1966 in California by Huey P Newton and Bobby Seale. The party was assembled to protect residents from police brutality. Black panther created 10 point program. The 10 point program included wanting freedom, full employment for African American people, ending robbery within their community, decent housing, not wanting Black men included in to draft of military, an end to police bruality, freedom for men incarcerated, and wanting equality in justice and peace.
